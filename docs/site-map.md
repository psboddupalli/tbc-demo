## Navigation Notes

- **"Educate"** replaces "TBC Institute" in the nav — more intuitive for visitors. The TBC Institute brand lives within the Fellowships page content.
- **"Connect"** is the umbrella for Events, News, and Resources — community-facing content.
- **"Innovate"** is a single page covering all three innovation areas: Capital, Land, Education.
- **"Invest"** covers both fundraising and sponsorship. The 2026 Sponsorship Invitation content lives here.
